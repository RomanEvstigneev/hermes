## What Axion Lab does

AI-native service firm for private markets. Builds AI co-pilots that assist analysts at PE firms, Private Credit funds, M&A advisories, and Insurance/Brokers in:
- Sustainability / ESG due diligence
- Investment memos (teaser, PIR, full memo)
- GHG / Scope 3 emissions analysis
- Sustainability value creation reports
- Document generation / DD automation

The positioning has evolved: started as AI-powered ESG reporting tool (Oct 2025), moved through value creation → due diligence → now settling on "AI co-pilot for private market due diligence and sustainability analysis." Theo (advisor) suggested framing as "ESG diligence co-pilot" or "Private market due diligence co-pilot."

Key differentiator framing: AI-native service firm (not SaaS), positioned to win over pure SaaS because people evaluate new things using old frameworks.
